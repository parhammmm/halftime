Remove football tweets from your timeline.

With the Worldcup, the twitter timeline can become a little cluttered, so we created this small extension that removes tweets about the Worldcup.

You can toggle it on or off in your twitter settings.

The tweets are not deleted but just hidden, so you can get them back any time by clicking "Show Football" in twitter's settings menu.
